How to use this page

Customer / Lease are optional and apply to all days below.

Next Day adds a new date and starts fresh with Crew #1.

Crews can be expanded or collapsed.

Trucks

  • Exactly 4 digits
  • Multiple trucks per crew allowed
  • You can add or remove trucks individually
  • You’ll be warned if a truck is reused on the same day

Rentals & Materials

Each line includes Item, Details, Qty/Distance, Vendor, and PO.

Example Output

OXY USA – SCR Lease

03/18/26
Crew #1
Trucks: 2145, 3057
John, Mike
Start: 0700   Stop: 1530
Installed pumps.

Low Pro – 600 HP – Qty 1 – Big D Pump – PO 12345
