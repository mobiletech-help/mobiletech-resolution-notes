How to use this page

Customer / Lease apply to all days.

Next Day adds a new date and resets to Crew #1.

Trucks

Enter all trucks taken to the location. Truck numbers must be exactly 4 digits. Duplicate trucks on the same day are not allowed.

Work Performed

Describe work completed during the day. Include delays, changes, or issues.

Rentals

Equipment left on location that will be returned later (pumps, generators, road crossings, automation, etc.).

Materials

Items or parts used or sold to the customer and not returned.
