How to use this page

This page is used to create clean, consistent resolution notes for MobileTech. Nothing is saved permanently — use Copy All to paste the notes into MobileTech when finished.

Customer / Lease

Customer and Lease / Location apply to all days entered below. Enter them once at the top unless they change. 

OXY USA – SCR LEASE

Days & Next Day

Each date represents a separate work day. Next Day adds a new date and starts fresh at Crew #1.

Crews

Each crew represents a group of employees working together. Multiple crews may be entered per day if needed.

Trucks

Enter all trucks taken to the location, even if only briefly onsite.

  • Truck numbers must be exactly 4 digits
  • Multiple trucks per crew are allowed
  • Duplicate trucks on the same day are blocked and identify the conflicting crew

Start / Stop Time

Enter the most accurate start and stop times possible. These times are used for billing and documentation.

Work Performed

Describe the work completed by the crew. This section protects the crew and company in the event of billing or scope questions.

  • Delays (weather, access, equipment, customer)
  • Change of scope
  • Added requests
  • Standby time
  • Unforeseen circumstances

Rentals (Equipment left on location – returning)

List equipment that was left on location and will be returned later. Do not include items that will be consumed or sold.

  • Pumps
  • Generators
  • Light towers
  • Automation
  • Road crossings
  • Flowmeters

Materials (Used or sold – not returning)

List materials or parts that were used or sold to the customer and will not be returned.

  • Liner
  • Geo
  • Heads
  • Fuel
  • Clamps
  • Fittings

Copy All

Copies only entered data into clean, MobileTech‑ready text. Empty sections are automatically omitted.
